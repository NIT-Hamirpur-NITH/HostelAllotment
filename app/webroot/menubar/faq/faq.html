FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS:

  1. Question:
    What if I forget to fill choices on time?
    Answer:
    This year choice filling will occur in phases i.e firstly for singlets then doublets and then triplets thus filling choice is necessary for all the students if they wish to participate in hostel allocation if how so ever you forget to or skip filling choices in any phase then you would not be allotted any room in that phase and you would be eligible to participate again in next phase.If you do not fill choices even in last phase you would be randomly allotted in that phase.


  2. Question:
    What if I fill about 20 choices and do not get any room due to my low rank ?
    Answer:
    The hostel allocation will first take place for singlet then doublets and then triplets, if you have filled 20 choices during singlet phase but did not get a room of choice then if a room remains then you will be allotted it otherwise you have to form groups for doublets and submit choices and if still not allotted then you repeat for triplets phase.


  3. Question:
    How many roommate requests can I send?
    Answer:
    You can send as many requests as you want but if accepted all other requests that were sent or received by the member of the group formed will be discarded.For doublet phase student with higher pointer will be the leader. For triplets the leader will handle further requests of both sending and receiving to form the group.


  4. Question:
    I am presently in 3rd year and I am eligible for DBH, VBH and MMH and my question is that will allotment for all these hostels begin together or one by one?
    Answer:
    This year allotment for all these hostels will begin together.



  5. Question:
    What if I do not form any group for doublets/triplets will I be able to fill choices or if we form incomplete group will our group still be eligible to fill choices?
    Answer:
    Yes you would be able to fill choices according to your rank or group leader rank in your incomplete group but you will later be accommodated in that room randomly with other students.
